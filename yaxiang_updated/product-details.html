产品概述

YXRF-3000系列全自动染色封片一体机是一款高度自动化的医疗器械设备，专为组织切片的染色和封片处理而设计。

该设备集成了先进的染色和封片技术，能够提供卓越的染色质量和封片效果，满足现代病理实验室的高标准要求。YXRF-3000系列不仅能保证出色的质量和稳定性，还能显著加快载玻片处理速度。

它能够确保从第1至2000张载玻片的染色效果始终如一，同时具备封片自动干片功能，干片时间仅需5分钟，为市场上最快的干片速度之一。染色封片完成后的载玻片迅速干燥可供后续使用，大大提高了实验室的工作效率。

  • 卓越的灵活性 - 提供独特的灵活性，与52个工作站点协作，实现高通量同步运行H&E染色和特殊染色。

  • 稳定可靠 - 采用先进的质量控制系统，确保染色和封片过程的一致性和可靠性。

Product Overview

The YXRF-3000 Series Automatic Stainer & Coverslipper is a highly automated medical device designed for tissue section staining and coverslipping.

This equipment integrates advanced staining and coverslipping technologies to provide excellent staining quality and coverslipping effects, meeting the high standards of modern pathology laboratories. The YXRF-3000 Series not only ensures outstanding quality and stability but also significantly accelerates slide processing speed.

It ensures consistent staining results from the 1st to the 2000th slide and features automatic slide drying with a drying time of just 5 minutes, one of the fastest drying speeds on the market. The rapidly dried slides after staining and coverslipping are immediately available for subsequent use, greatly improving laboratory efficiency.

Key Features

  • Efficient Intelligent Processing - Processes up to 570 dried slides per hour, quickly completing the coverslipping process and accelerating the diagnostic process.
  • Outstanding Flexibility - Offers unique flexibility, collaborating with 52 workstations to achieve high-throughput synchronous operation of H&E staining and special staining.
  • Intelligent Operation - Equipped with an intuitive touch screen interface, simplifying operation procedures, reducing training time, and improving work efficiency.
  • Stable and Reliable - Employs an advanced quality control system to ensure consistency and reliability in the staining and coverslipping process.

全面的功能特点

高速处理能力

YXRF-3000B每小时可处理多达570张载玻片，大大提高了实验室的工作效率。其独特的设计允许同时进行染色和封片操作，无需等待染色完成后再进行封片，节省了宝贵的时间。

多样化染色协议

支持多达50种不同的染色协议，包括H&E染色、特殊染色和免疫组化染色。用户可以根据需要自定义染色步骤、时间和试剂，满足不同类型组织样本的染色需求。

安全保障系统

配备多重安全保障系统，包括试剂泄漏检测、温度监控、载玻片防碰撞保护等。当检测到异常情况时，系统会立即发出警报并采取相应措施，确保设备和样本的安全。

智能控制系统

采用先进的智能控制系统，配备10.4英寸高清触摸屏，操作界面直观友好。系统支持多语言界面，可实时监控设备状态、试剂用量和处理进度，并提供详细的操作日志和错误提示。

试剂管理系统

内置智能试剂管理系统，可自动监控试剂使用情况，提醒用户及时更换或补充试剂。系统还能根据使用频率和时间自动计算试剂的有效期，确保染色质量。

网络连接功能

支持网络连接，可与实验室信息系统(LIS)和医院信息系统(HIS)无缝集成。通过网络接口，用户可以远程监控设备状态、上传染色协议、下载操作日志，实现数据的集中管理和分析。

Comprehensive Features

High-Speed Processing

The YXRF-3000B can process up to 570 slides per hour, greatly improving laboratory efficiency. Its unique design allows simultaneous staining and coverslipping operations, eliminating the need to wait for staining to complete before coverslipping, saving valuable time.

Diverse Staining Protocols

Supports up to 50 different staining protocols, including H&E staining, special staining, and immunohistochemical staining. Users can customize staining steps, times, and reagents according to their needs, meeting the staining requirements of different types of tissue samples.

Safety Protection System

Equipped with multiple safety protection systems, including reagent leak detection, temperature monitoring, slide collision protection, etc. When abnormal conditions are detected, the system immediately issues an alarm and takes corresponding measures to ensure the safety of equipment and samples.

Intelligent Control System

Adopts an advanced intelligent control system with a 10.4-inch high-definition touch screen and an intuitive, user-friendly interface. The system supports multiple languages, can monitor equipment status, reagent usage, and processing progress in real-time, and provides detailed operation logs and error prompts.

Reagent Management System

Built-in intelligent reagent management system that automatically monitors reagent usage and reminds users to replace or replenish reagents in a timely manner. The system can also automatically calculate the shelf life of reagents based on frequency and time of use, ensuring staining quality.

Network Connectivity

Supports network connectivity for seamless integration with Laboratory Information Systems (LIS) and Hospital Information Systems (HIS). Through the network interface, users can remotely monitor device status, upload staining protocols, download operation logs, and achieve centralized data management and analysis.
